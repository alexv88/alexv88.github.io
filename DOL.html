Department of Labor

Senior UX Designer

Due to the sensitive nature of some projects with the Department of Labor (DOL), I am only allowed to show certain pieces

One of the projects I worked on when I was with Astor and Sanders was the Unemployment Insurance programs through the Department of Labor. Each state had their own requirements, needs, and wants. I worked with the stakeholders one-on-one and keep them in the design processes while wireframes were created and moved to high fidelity mocks once the functionality, user flows, and initial layout was approved.

>Responsive design was a key factor in functionality and meeting the needs of the users. Users needed a good experience both web versions and mobile. Working with developers, I was a key part in bridging the gap and creating a successful user experience and user interface.
