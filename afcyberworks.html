Due to the sensitive nature of some projects with the Department of Defense (DoD), I am only allowed to show certain pieces.

AF CyberWorX is the Air Force's first innovation hub that is held at the Air Force Academy were human centered design is integrated into daily classes. While I was apart of AF Cyberworx I mentored cadets with their capstone classes applying design thinking processes, designed the AF CyberWorX brand, worked with notable networks such as AT&T and conducted design sprints for various governmental departments.

UX/UI Position, Navigation, and Timing

Position, navigation, and timing (PNT) is the ability to located ones position and orientation with a desired location throughout the word and acquire accurte time. When this is combined with map data we get the popular GPS.

National Air and Space Museum, Smithsonian Institution, Bruce Morser on timeandnavigation.si.edu

One of projects that I worked on with the cadets was PNT and our military. This concept is nothing new to our militry and we are finding better ways to improve this process. During this capstone, myself and the cadets tested theories, interviewed, tested, and worked with various departments.
